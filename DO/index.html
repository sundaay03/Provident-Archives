Darussalam Olympiad

Darussalam Olympiad is an annual competition event held at Pondok Modern Darussalam, serving as a platform to develop students’ intellectual abilities, mental resilience, sportsmanship, and character.

The event includes various competitions in academics, sports, languages, arts, and skills, designed to foster a healthy competitive spirit, teamwork, discipline, and leadership. Darussalam Olympiad also serves as a practical application of the Five Spirits especially sincerity, self-reliance, and Islamic brotherhood—within a dynamic and inspiring atmosphere.
